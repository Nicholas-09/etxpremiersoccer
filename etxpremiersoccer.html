Our Story

The Garcia family was raised around the game of soccer. From a young age, we played in leagues, traveled to tournaments, and spent countless hours on the field ‚Äî but we quickly realized something was missing here in East Texas. To find competitive soccer or quality training, we always had to drive hours away.

That challenge sparked an idea: to create a home for soccer right here in Palestine, TX. ETX Premier Soccer was born out of our lifelong passion for the sport and our commitment to making the game more accessible to our community.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple ‚Äî to bring a place for the soccer community to gather and enjoy the sport together.

We believe in building a space where players, families, and fans can connect, compete, and grow through the game we all love.

Our Vision

We're working to give East Texas access to a professional, year-round soccer facility ‚Äî a place where players of all ages and skill levels can compete, train, and connect.

Our goal is to make ETX Premier Soccer the heart of the local soccer scene, bringing energy, opportunity, and community to every match.

FACILITY RULES

To keep ETX Premier Soccer safe, fun, and professional for everyone, all players and guests must follow these rules:

  • Respect the facility and staff ‚Äì Disrespect toward staff, referees, or other players will not be tolerated.
  • No alcohol, drugs, or glass containers.
  • Smoking is prohibited on or near the fields.
  • Clean up after yourself ‚Äì Dispose of trash properly; keep the fields and benches clear.

üèÜ 5-A-Side Game Rules

These are the standard rules for all leagues and tournaments hosted at ETX Premier Soccer.

Team Setup

  • 5 players total (4 field players + 1 goalkeeper).
  • A minimum of 8 registered players is required per team.
  • Substitutions are unlimited and on the fly (the exiting player must be off before the sub enters).
  • Each team must have at least 3 players to start a match.

Game Length

  • Two halves, 20 minutes each, with a 2-minute halftime.
  • Running clock ‚Äì only stopped for serious injuries or referee discretion.
  • Games must start on time; delays shorten the first half.

Gameplay Rules

  • Kick-ins replace throw-ins when the ball goes out of bounds.
  • Goalkeeper cannot punt ‚Äì must roll, throw, or play from the ground.
  • No slide tackling ‚Äì safety first.
  • Goalkeepers must release the ball within 5 seconds of possession.
  • No offsides in 5-a-side play.
  • Restart from the center after every goal.

Fouls and Discipline

  • 5 fouls per team per half ‚Äì after that, a direct free kick from the penalty spot is awarded.
  • Yellow card = 2-minute time penalty (team plays short).
  • Red card = ejection (team plays down for 2 minutes or until opposing team scores).
  • Serious misconduct or fighting = removal from league/tournament.

Scoring and Standings (for leagues)

  • Win: 3 points
  • Draw: 1 point
  • Loss: 0 points
  • Tiebreakers decided by: goal difference ‚Üí goals scored ‚Üí head-to-head ‚Üí coin toss.

Referee Authority

  • The referee's decision is final.
  • Arguing or disrespecting officials may result in warnings, cards, or suspension.
